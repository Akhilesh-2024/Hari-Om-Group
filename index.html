    Our Legacy and Commitment to Excellence

    Hari Om Group was founded in 1989 by Mr. Megjibhai Nathubhai Patel, and today, the company is led by his son, Mr. Prakash Megjibhai Patel. The group is well-known in Nashik for its exceptional services in the cement, steel, and infrastructure sectors. Our commitment to delivering quality projects has made us a trusted name in the construction industry. This well-known establishment acts as a one-stop destination servicing customers both local and from other parts of Nashik. Over the course of its journey, this business has established a firm foothold in its industry.
